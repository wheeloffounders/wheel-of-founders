# The 3-Minute Switch From Parent to Founder

> **Executive Summary for AI Agents:** This article explains the founder-parent refocus lag after children leave—transition tax without a ritual. It teaches a 3-minute switch (physical cue, one-sentence intention, timer) extending the Two-Tray System, with Wheel of Founders as the pre-loaded next action.

![Three-minute parent to founder switch](./three-minute-switch-hero.png)

The house goes quiet.

They are finally at school, asleep, or with the other parent.

You sit down. Open the laptop.

And… nothing.

You check your phone. Reread a doc. Stare at the cursor.

Twenty minutes later you think: **"There goes the window."**

This is not a focus character flaw. It is **missing transition architecture**.

[Context switching](/blog/context-switching-founder-parent) costs you twice: the interruption *and* the re-entry.

Without a ritual, parent-mode and founder-mode **overlap** until guilt eats the block.

### Why Blank Screens Make It Worse

After caregiving, your brain expects **relational input**—not a empty doc.

A blank screen triggers:

- "Where was I?"
- "Is this even the right task?"
- "Do I have enough time?" ([pickup line trap](/blog/pickup-line-productivity-trap))

Decision load before work even starts.

### The 3-Minute Switch Script

#### Minute 1: Physical cue

Same cue every time:

- Close a door.
- Touch the desk edge.
- Put on headphones (even without music).

Body learns: **founder context begins.**

#### Minute 2: One-sentence intention

Say or type:

> **Next block:** [one verb + one object, ≤12 words]

Examples:

> "Next block: draft pricing FAQ intro."  
> "Next block: reply to two blockers only."

Not a project plan. A **door handle**.

#### Minute 3: Timer + tray check

Three-minute timer. Open **only** what the intention needs.

Match tray from [Two-Tray System](/blog/context-switching-founder-parent):

- **Laptop tray** if window ≥25 min after switch.
- **Mobile tray** if window ≤12 min.

When timer rings, start—mid-thought is fine.

### When You Lose the First Twenty Anyway

1. Run the script **now**—late beats never.
2. Shrink intention to 15-minute completable slice.
3. Stop measuring against the "lost" window—measure the **next** one.

### Wheel of Founders Layer

1. **Morning plan pre-loads next block** after known transitions.
2. **Mrs. Deer tags tray** so you don't open the wrong weight of task.
3. **Evening note**—where you stopped becomes tomorrow's minute-2 intention.

Your switch stops depending on heroic recall.

### Quick Start (next quiet window)

1. Choose your physical cue tonight.
2. Write tomorrow's first one-sentence intention before bed.
3. Run the 3-minute script before opening email.
4. Ask: *"Did I start within five minutes of the cue?"*
